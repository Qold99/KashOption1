Service policy

Official Terms • Global Logistics Policy

01. Operational scope

Kash Option operates exclusively as a Professional Sourcing & Logistics Facilitator. By engaging our services, you acknowledge:

  • We are a third-party bridge and not the manufacturer of sourced goods.
  • We hold no local inventory; items are procured on-demand from global markets.
  • Manufacturer warranties are governed by the brand, not by Kash Option.

02. Financial terms

  • Quote Validity: International prices fluctuate. Quotes are strictly valid for 48 hours.
  • Procurement: No item is purchased or reserved until 100% full payment is confirmed.
  • Local Payments: We accept MoMo, Bank Transfer, and Cards. Once a supplier is paid, funds are non-refundable.

03. Logistics & timelines

Timelines are estimates starting from the date the item reaches our international hub:

  • Air Cargo: 3 to 5 working days.
  • Sea Freight: 6 to 8 weeks.
  • Customs: We manage clearing, but clients are responsible for any government revaluation fees mandated by GRA.

04. Finality of sale

Non-returnable policy

Due to the complexity of international logistics, returns to China, USA, or UK are not possible once the item has cleared customs into Ghana.

  • Exchanges are only considered for Verified Factory Defects reported within 48 hours of receipt.
  • Return shipping costs for approved defect claims are the responsibility of the buyer.
  • No Cash Refunds: Sales are final. Credits may be issued for approved claims.

05. Technical liability

  • Auto Parts: Fitment is your responsibility. Misdiagnosis by your mechanic is not a valid claim for exchange.
  • Electronics: We are not liable for damage caused by local power surges or incorrect voltage application.

By making payment to Kash Option, you agree to these terms in full.
