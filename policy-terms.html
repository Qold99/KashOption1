What We Do

Kash Option is a sourcing and logistics service for customers in Ghana. We help you buy products from overseas (China, USA, UK, Europe), ship them by air or sea, clear customs, and deliver to your address. We coordinate the purchase when you ask us to, or we can ship items you have already bought from your own supplier. We are not the manufacturer of any products and do not provide a manufacturer warranty unless we say so in writing.

If any part of these terms conflicts with the law where you live, that part will apply only to the fullest extent the law allows.

How Your Order Works

You send us an inquiry (product, quantity, specs). We give you a quote that includes product cost and shipping to Ghana. When you accept and pay in full, we place the order with the supplier and arrange shipping. You receive an order ID and tracking when your item is on the way. Delivery is door-to-door in Ghana where we offer it. Full payment is required before we buy or ship; we do not hold or reserve items without payment.

Inventory and Sourcing From Abroad

We do not keep a physical store or warehouse of products in Ghana. Everything we source is purchased from suppliers and platforms abroad and shipped to you. Prices and availability depend on those suppliers, exchange rates, and shipping options. We work with trusted suppliers and do basic checks where we can (packaging, visible condition, quantity), but we cannot guarantee every detail of a product that we did not manufacture. You are responsible for confirming the exact specs you need (model, size, color, compatibility, voltage, region) before paying.

Shipping timelines are estimates and depend on supplier handling, carrier schedules, customs, peak seasons, and external events.
