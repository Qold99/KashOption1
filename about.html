How It Works

Clear steps from your first message to delivery.

1. Inquire

Tell us what you want. Share product links, photos, or descriptions. We’ll confirm availability and send you a clear quote.

2. Pay

Full payment secures your order. We use secure channels. Once payment is received, we purchase and prepare your items.

3. Ship

We ship by air (about 3 to 5 working days) or sea (about 6 to 8 weeks). You get tracking details. We handle customs and documentation.

4. Receive

Your package is delivered to your chosen address in Ghana. Door-to-door, with no hidden import charges when quoted upfront.
