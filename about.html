The founder's story

Kash Option was founded by Prince Okyere to solve a major problem: shopping abroad from Ghana was broken.

By removing obstacles like unreliable suppliers and complex customs hurdles, we transformed international sourcing into a simple WhatsApp message. Today, we are Ghana's most trusted logistics gateway.

The journey

2020: The launch

Started in Accra with a focus on high-speed China sourcing for small businesses.

2021: Global expansion

Activated USA and UK logistics hubs, opening Western markets to the Ghanaian consumer.

2024: The #1 standard

Recognized as the premier procurement and logistics standard for elite global sourcing.
