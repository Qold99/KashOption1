The Founder's Story

Kash Option was founded by Prince Okyere with a clear mission: to end the frustration of international shopping for Ghanaians.

By tackling unreliable suppliers, eliminating hidden fees, and navigating complex customs, we transformed global sourcing from a headache into a seamless doorstep delivery service. We don't just ship boxes; we move businesses forward.

The Journey
